Research & Engineering Focus

Physics‑Informed Constitutive Modeling

Built interpretable neural networks (ICNNs) to learn stress–strain behavior consistent with classical elasticity (energy positivity, symmetry, positive‑definiteness). Used dimensionality reduction and symbolic regression to extract stiffness constants (e.g., C11, C12, C22, C66).

Surrogate Modeling for Systems

Created data‑driven reduced‑order models from high‑fidelity simulations to accelerate system‑level analysis and design iteration. Methods include Bayesian optimization for hyperparameters and uncertainty estimation.

Simulation
