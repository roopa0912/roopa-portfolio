Linkage Mechanism

The linkage mechanism is a simple assembly demonstrating motion transfer between connected rigid bodies. Designed in SolidWorks, this project highlights constraints, mates, and assembly relationships used to simulate mechanical motion.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Assembly mates, pin connections, motion simulation
  • Applications: Demonstrates motion transfer in mechanical linkages
