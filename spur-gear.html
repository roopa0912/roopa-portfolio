Spur Gear

The spur gear is one of the most common types of gears, widely used in mechanical systems for power transmission. This model was designed in SolidWorks, focusing on gear geometry and manufacturability.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Extruded Boss/Base, Circular Pattern, Fillet
  • Applications: Power transmission in gear trains
