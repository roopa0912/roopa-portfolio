Rubber Mat

The Rubber Mat was designed in SolidWorks to demonstrate patterned features and surface detailing. Mats like this are commonly used in gyms, workshops, and homes for flooring, vibration absorption, and durability.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Extruded Boss/Base, Linear Pattern, Chamfer
  • Applications: Flooring mats for gyms, workshops, household use
