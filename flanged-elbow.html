Flanged Pipe Elbow

The flanged pipe elbow is a connector component used in piping systems to redirect flow at an angle. It was designed in SolidWorks, demonstrating modeling of cylindrical geometry, angled extrusions, and circular hole patterns for bolted flange connections.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Revolved Boss/Base, Extruded Cut, Circular Pattern, Fillet
  • Applications: Used in piping and fluid transport systems for angled connections
