Cupcake Tray

The Cupcake Tray is modeled to replicate bakeware commonly used in kitchens. Designed in SolidWorks, it demonstrates cavity design using revolve and pattern features, along with shelling operations for thin walls.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Revolved Cut, Shell, Circular Pattern
  • Applications: Kitchen bakeware, mold design demonstration
