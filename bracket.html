Machined Bracket

This machined bracket was designed in SolidWorks to practice creating common machining features. The design includes mounting holes, angled faces, and support geometry typical of mechanical support blocks. It demonstrates an understanding of machining-friendly geometry and parametric modeling.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Extruded Boss/Base, Cut-Extrude (holes), Chamfer, Fillet
  • Applications: Mounting block / support component in assemblies
