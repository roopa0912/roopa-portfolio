Universal Joint

The Universal Joint (also called a U-joint or Cardan joint) is a mechanical coupling that allows shafts to transmit torque at varying angles. It’s widely used in automotive drive shafts, industrial machinery, and mechanical systems where flexibility of power transmission is required. This model was created in SolidWorks, focusing on kinematic behavior and design efficiency.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Revolve, Extruded Boss/Base, Assembly Mates, Fillets
  • Applications: Power transmission in automotive drive shafts, industrial machines, and equipment requiring rotation at an angle
