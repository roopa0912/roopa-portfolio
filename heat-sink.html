Heat Sink

The heat sink is a thermal management component commonly used in electronics and mechanical systems to dissipate heat. This design, modeled in SolidWorks, demonstrates the use of extrusion and linear patterning tools to create fins for increased surface area, improving heat transfer.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Extruded Boss/Base, Linear Pattern, Fillet
  • Applications: Cooling of electronic components and mechanical systems
