Cylinder–Piston Linkage

This Cylinder–Piston Linkage demonstrates how reciprocating motion from a piston can be transmitted through a linkage system. The assembly highlights interaction between sliding and rotating members, making it a classic example of motion transfer in mechanical systems. Designed in SolidWorks, it focuses on kinematic behavior and practical applications in engines and actuators.

Design Details

  • Software Used: SolidWorks
  • Features Applied: Extruded Boss/Base, Slot, Assembly Mates (Slider, Pin, Revolute)
  • Applications: Demonstration of piston–linkage motion transfer in engines, actuators, and reciprocating mechanisms
